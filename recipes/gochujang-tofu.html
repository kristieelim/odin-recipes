Gochujang Tofu

Photo source: Pick Up Limes

This is a delicious vegan tofu recipe by my favourite Youtuber, Pick Up Limes.
It is spicy, sweet and garlicky. This dish uses Gochujang, a spicy fermented condiment popular in Korean cuisine. I enjoy this dish with rice.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Corn Starch
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Gochujang
  • Maple Syrup
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Spring Onion
  • Chilli

Steps

  1. Cut two blocks of tofu into bite size pieces.
  2. Coat the tofu with corn starch.
  3. Fry the coated tofu with some vegetable oil in a pan on medium heat until golden.
  4. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside for later.
  5. Chop five cloves of garlic, one shallot, two spring onions and one chilli into small pieces.
  6. Saute the garlic, shallots, spring onion and chilli in a pan with vegetable oil on medium heat until aromatic.
  7. To make the sauce, mix two tablespoons of maple syrup and one tablespoon each of gochujang, soy sauce and rice vinegar in a bowl.
  8. Add the sauce to the pan with the sauteed aromatics. Let it cook for about a minute.
  9. Add the tofu to the pan and stir the dish to coat the tofu with the sauce.
  10. Serve with freshly cooked jasmine rice!
