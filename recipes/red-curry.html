Red Curry

Photo source: Pick Up Limes

A very versatile dish. Just use whatever vegetables you have in your fridge!
The vegetables in the ingredient list are just our suggestions. Feel free to swap with any vegetables you have on hand.
The chickpeas can be substituted for any other protein of your choice.
Best enjoyed with jasmine rice. Caution: can be spicy!

Ingredients

  • Thai red curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Zucchini
  • Carrot
  • Jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Cook the jasmine rice in a rice cooker.
  2. Chop one onion into small pieces
  3. Cut all the vegetables to bite sized pieces to your liking.
  4. Saute the chopped onion with vegetable oil in a pot on medium heat.
  5. Reduce to low heat and add one tablespoon of thai red curry paste.
  6. Add coconut milk, water and the vegetables and let it boil until the vegetables are cooked.
  7. Add the chickpeas.
  8. Serve with jasmine rice.
